## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: Purple (#9333EA)
- Secondary: Pink (#EC4899)
- Accent: Blue (#3B82F6)
- Background: Dark (#111827)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF)

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, 48-72pt
- Body: Regular, 24-32pt
- Code: Monospace, 18-24pt

### Layout
- Consistent margins (10% on all sides)
- High contrast for readability
- Minimal text per slide
- Strong visual hierarchy

### Animations
- Subtle fade-ins
- Smooth transitions
- No distracting effects
- Professional polish

5. **Clear Value** - Always tie back to problem/solution
6. **Confident Delivery** - Practice timing (5-7 minutes)
7. **Handle Questions** - Prepare for technical deep-dives
